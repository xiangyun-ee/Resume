Overview

My research focuses on algorithm development and integrative mining from high throughput data to understand gene regulation, function in model organism. I have developed the algorithms for transcription factor motif finding, mutagenesis-seq data analysis. Through integrating genome-wide transcription factor binding, chromatin dynamics, gene expression profiles, and chemical and functional screens, we try to model the specificity and function of transcription factors, chromatin regulators, RNA binding proteins, kinases, and lncRNAs in Plasmodium life cycle, progression, drug response and resistance.
